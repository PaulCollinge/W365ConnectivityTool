## Windows 365 / AVD Local Connectivity Scanner

Download `W365LocalScanner.exe` and run it on the client machine.
It will perform local-only diagnostics and automatically open the web diagnostics page with results.

### Verify integrity

SHA256: `08A5B527A0445C7399BFDD900758E5F42CA6E0AA3530C0626BBAF9775EDFF1B2`

`powershell
# Verify after download:
(Get-FileHash W365LocalScanner.exe -Algorithm SHA256).Hash
``n
### Verify build provenance

``ngh attestation verify W365LocalScanner.exe --repo PaulCollinge/W365ConnectivityTool
``n
